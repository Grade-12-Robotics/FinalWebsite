About ESP8266


What is an Esp8266?


The ESP8266 can be controlled from your local Wi-Fi network or from the internet. The ESP-01 module has GPIO pins that can be programmed to turn an LED or a relay ON/OFF through the internet. The module can be programmed using an Arduino/USB-to-TTL converter through the serial pins (RX,TX).
